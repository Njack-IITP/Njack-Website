  • NJATH

NJATH

Not Just Another Treasure Hunt

The name says it all. Its a Treasure hunt unlike anything you've ever seen.

General Rules:

  1. NJATH is an online treasure hunt contest. You will be given questions and you will need to decipher the solution.
  2. Lookout for clues and hints anywhere from url of page to all details in the question . You are allowed to use internet . You can also download question image if necessary
  3. For any question, there is no limitation for number of attempts.
  4. There are treasure chest hidden throughout the hunts, they will provide additional points and bonus multipliers.
  5. Discuss forum can be used for extra hints but should not disclose the answer.
  6. There are 6 levels in the hunt. Each level contains 8 questions in which 2 questions are bonus questions.
  7. Once advanced to a new level, you cannot go to the previous level.



Catch you guys on the leaderboard. Happy Hunting

Mind Benders

Mind-benders is a test of wits and logic. The event involves no syllabus or pre-requisite knowledge. The event will solely test the student's logical reasoning, analytical thinking and problem-solving capabilities.

General Rules:

  1. This is a team event. Students should participate in teams of two members.
  2. Students of different institutions can form a team.
  3. The event will be in form of pen-paper quiz, it will consist of single choice correct, multiple choice correct and paragraph questions with different marking schemes for each section.
  4. Use of mobile phones or technology are strictly prohibited during the test.
